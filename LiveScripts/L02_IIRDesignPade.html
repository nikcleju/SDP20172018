The Pade method

In the Pade method, we want to design the filter such that the impulse response is similar to a desired impulse response .
The desired impulse response can be, for example, the impulse reponse of an ideal low-pass, band-pass or high-pass filter (which needs a filter of infinite order so can't be implemented exactly).
